# personas-demo Research

This demo is grounded in research on enterprise persona systems, schema rigor, and UI/UX for decision support tools. Key research and design principles:

- **Schema Rigor:** The persona schema is designed for auditability, evidence tracking, and extensibility, supporting enterprise needs for traceability and compliance.
- **Progressive Disclosure:** The UI uses progressive disclosure to avoid overwhelming users, revealing details as needed.
- **Compact UI:** Minimal spacing, left-aligned headings, and emoji cues ensure information density and readability.
- **Evidence & Audit Trail:** Each persona includes evidence sources, validation status, and falsification criteria for transparency.
- **Customization:** The structure and UI are easily extensible for additional persona attributes or enterprise requirements.
